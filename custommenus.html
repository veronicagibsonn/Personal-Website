Custom Menus Using Google Apps Script

Using Google Apps Script, I've created custom menus in the Google Workspace—formerly known as G Suite to solve common business problems that can be automated (i.e. searches/filtering, sorting, counting, lists, personalization, robustness, security and persistence). What people don't realize is how much value is left untouched in Google App Script's capabilities, especially in data mature companies. Whether it's emailing clients within a certain demographic and/or coordinating meetings with C‐Suite executives, oftentimes many companies find themselves in situations where they lose time from completing these tasks manually. Using custom menus within the Google Workspace (Google Sheets, Slides, Docs, Forms) I've automated and streamlined tedious and mundane tasks, creating additional value by increasing productivity and providing ease of use to any and all end users. Essentially, anyone from your team can run and benefit from your code, as the interface you've created for them is extremely easy‐to‐use
